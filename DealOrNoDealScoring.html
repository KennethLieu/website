Deal or No Deal Scoring:

If you accept ANY deal from banker: your score = $ amount from banker
If you reject ALL deals from banker, but decide to switch your case with the last remaining case: your score = $ amount from other case
If you reject ALL deals from banker, but decide to keep your case: your score = $ amount from your case
If you decide to take mystery case: your score = $ amount from round * multiplier
If you decide NOT to take mystery case: your score = $ amount from round
