Hangman Rules:

The game will start off with 2 players deciding whether they wish to be player 1 (word generator) or player 2 (guesser). Afterwards, player 1 would need to come up with a word or phrase, and player 2 would have to guess letter by letter before they use up all their guesses. Player 2 wins when they successfully reveals the word in the amount of guesses they have left. Otherwise, player 1 will win. Afterwards, both players have the option to play again or not. The winner will be determined based on total number of points. If tied, winner will be by random draw.
